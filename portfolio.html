    VR Ward Project - Final Year Project

    I explored VR development further and also a whole new industry — the medical sector. The simulation helps students train in ward environments by diagnosing patients with different conditions.

    What I Learned

    • Optimizing VR experiences for hardware and user comfort.
    • Designing interactive simulations in Unity.
    • Integrating external datasets into gameplay systems.

    Key Takeaways

    • Real-world projects present extra design challenges.
    • Accessibility and comfort are as important as visuals in VR.
    • Cross-discipline collaboration is crucial for serious simulations.
