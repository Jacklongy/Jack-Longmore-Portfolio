How We Work Together

Bring Your Game Idea to Life

We start by discussing your game idea in detail. I'll listen to your vision, understand your goals, and ask questions to clarify what you're trying to achieve.

Once we understand the concept, we'll break it down into a nicely scoped project. This means defining what features are essential for the first version, what can wait for later updates, and what's realistic given your budget and timeline.

Together, we'll decide on a basic timeframe for your project. This is flexible and will depend on the scope and the contract we agree on. Whether you need a quick prototype or a fully polished game, we'll work out what works best for you.
